Lasagna

Lasagna is a delicious comforting dish love by people of all kind and cats of the orange kind. This recipe will teach you to make a great lasagna to feed your family or a small town.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs ground beef
  • 1 lb Italian sausage
  • 8 oz mushrooms, chopped
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 3/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper
  • 6 cups marinara
  • 16 oz lasagna noodles
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 lbs ricotta
  • 8 oz mozzarella
  • 1 cup parmigiano reggiano
  • 1/4 chopped parsley
  • 1 pinch cayenne

steps

  1. Cook meat 10 minutes and stir in mushrooms. Cook until dry.
  2. Add sauce to the meat mixture. Simmer for 2 hours.
  3. Boil pasta for 8 minutes when sauce is almost finished.
  4. Whisk eggs, cheeses and parsley to make filling.
    5. preheat oven to 375 degrees.
  5. layer sauce noodles and filling until pan is full.
  6. cover with aluminum foil for 30 minutes covered, 35 uncovered.

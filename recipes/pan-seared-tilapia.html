Pan-Seared Tilapia

These tender tilapia are quick and easy. Perfect for an easy weeknight dinner.

Ingredients

  • 4 tilapia fillets
  • salt to taste
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsps unsalted butter
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp parsley
  • 1/2 tsp chopped thyme

Steps

  1. Rinse tilapia fillets and pat dry, season with salt and pepper.
  2. Place flour in dish, press fillets in flour and shake off excess.
  3. Heat olive oil in skillet over medium high heat. Cook about 4 minutes per side.
  4. Brush with melted butter on last minute of cooking.
  5. Garnish with lemon juice, parsley, and thyme.

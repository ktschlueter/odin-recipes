Quesadilla

A simple quesadilla recipe. Enjoy dinner within 30 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken breast strips
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 onion chopped
  • 2 tbsps salsa
  • 10 tortillas
  • 2 cups monterey jack cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Fry chicken strips until no longer pink.
  3. Add onions and fry until translucent, mix in salsa.
  4. Microwave tortillas for 1 minute.
  5. Fill tortillas with chicken and cheese.
  6. Bake until cheese has melted and serve.
